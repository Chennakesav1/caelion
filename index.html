Engineering Core

Legacy Modernization

Strangler-fig decomposition of legacy monoliths into scalable, cloud-native microservices. We modernize without business disruption.

Safe, Incremental Refactoring

We do not believe in "big bang" rewrites. Our modernization strategy isolates core business domains, wrapping them in new APIs, and slowly strangling the legacy systems out of existence while functionality remains completely online. This prevents total system halts common in major manufacturing and logistics facilities.

  • Monolith to Microservices decomposition
  • On-premise to Cloud migration (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Database migration and zero-downtime synchronization
